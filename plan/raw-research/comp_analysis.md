# Candidate 1: Iron Bank 2

## What Works
- Background music plays only within the browser tab. If you switch or close tabs, the music stops.
- The usual slot machine features are all present and functional.
- Long explanation and paytable rules are placed on a separate page.
- Must click a “more / 3-horizontal lines” button to access full details.
- Can toggle all sounds including sound effects and music.
- Keyboard functionality is toggleable.
- Splash screen special effects for entertainment.
- Two additional gameplay-changing features:
  - **Bonus Reels** (costs increased bet amount, removes regular symbols)
  - **Enhanced Spins** (increases chance of a hit)

## What Doesn’t Work
- "Show History" button at the bottom of the Rules page does not work.
- Splash screen effects may slow down players who want faster gameplay.
- No cumulative tracker for total winnings.
- Toggle feature labeled **W / ?** is unclear in function.

## Notable Features
- Gamemode selection before entering:
  - Random Mystery Symbols
  - Random Bonus Symbols
  - Full Wild Reels
  - Mystery Symbols
  - Expanding Wild
  - Multiplier Connect
- Autoplay feature (0, 10, 25, 50, 100, 250, 500, 1000, infinite spins)
- Speed-up button for faster spins
- Free spins (dependent on game mode)
- Store for purchasing free spins
